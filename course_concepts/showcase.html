Concept 01
Reducing Algorithmic Control
Travel discovery is dominated by curated feeds that feel empowering but hide algorithmic bias. TripTailor replaces ranked lists with a map interface — giving users spatial freedom to explore on their own terms, not the platform's.
🗺 Map-first exploration
→
Concept 02
Socio-Technical Systems
This is not just an interface problem. TripTailor weaves together local knowledge, urban mobility, budgeting, platform discovery, and user behavior into a single, coherent design challenge.
⚙️ Systems thinking
→
Concept 03
Affordances
Every action — browsing, adding stops, viewing budgets, comparing transport, following a guide — is designed to feel immediately visible and understandable without instruction.
👁 Perceived affordances
→
