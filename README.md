## Project Overview

TripTailor is a student-built concept for AI-assisted travel planning, designed to bridge the gap between travel inspiration and actual planning. The platform transforms saved social media posts, bookmarks, and travel ideas into structured, budget-conscious routes with integrated transit and food recommendations.

## Key Features

- **From Inspiration to Action**: Turn scattered travel bookmarks into actionable plans
- **Budget-First Planning**: Know your costs upfront before you go
- **Integrated Transit & Food**: Seamless route planning with local food suggestions
- **AI-Powered Assistance**: Intelligent route optimization based on your preferences
- **Local-Focused Discovery**: Curated spots beyond typical tourist attractions

## Target Audience

- **Students**: Exploring on tight budgets with limited time
- **Newcomers & Visitors**: Navigating unfamiliar cities without local knowledge  
- **Families**: Coordinating group outings with mixed preferences
- **Budget-Conscious Explorers**: Maximizing experiences while minimizing costs
- **Local Residents**: Discovering more of their own city
